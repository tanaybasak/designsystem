Use of the notice informs the public that a work is protected by copyright, identifies the copyright owner and shows the year of first publiction. Furthermore in the event that a work is infringed, if the work carries a proper notice, the court will not give any weight to a defendant's use of an innocent infringement defense- that is, to a claim that the defendant did not realize that the work was protected.
